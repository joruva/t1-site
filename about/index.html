About

A career spent building products people trust.

T1 Consulting is led by Thomas J. Russo — former President & Chief Executive Officer of First Alert, the most-recognized home-safety brand in North America.

Thomas J. Russo
Principal · T1 Consulting LLC

Thomas J. Russo spent fifteen years as President and Chief Executive Officer of First Alert before founding T1 Consulting in 2026 to advise companies building physical products.

Under his leadership, First Alert manufactured and marketed the smoke alarms, carbon-monoxide alarms, fire extinguishers, and connected home-safety products that protect tens of millions of American households. The business was sold to Resideo Technologies (NYSE: REZI) for $590 million in February 2022, completing a multi-year transition from Newell Brands' Connected Home & Security division into an independent public-company subsidiary.

A panoramic view of a category

Tom joined First Alert after a senior career at Kidde — Carrier's fire-safety brand and First Alert's principal North American competitor — where he held general-manager responsibilities across marketing and sales. Moving from the general management of one major brand to the presidency of the other gave him a vantage on the residential and commercial product category that few operators in any industry can match.

During his tenure, First Alert held category leadership in residential smoke and carbon-monoxide alarms, combination alarms, and ten-year sealed alarms — a category First Alert defined when it brought the first such product to market — as well as connected, Wi-Fi-enabled alarms through the OneLink platform launched under his leadership. He led the company through Newell Brands' acquisition of Jarden in 2016, the integration into Newell's Connected Home & Security division, the 2018 acquisition of Luma Home that brought mesh Wi-Fi into the OneLink platform, and the eventual divestiture to Resideo that returned the business to standalone operating status.

Built and run at scale

First Alert's North American manufacturing platform — centered in Aurora, Illinois with plants in Juarez, Mexico — operated at scale throughout that period. The result is a leader who has lived the entire arc of a physical-product business: concept and certification, manufacturing and sourcing, channel and category strategy, and the mergers, integrations, and divestiture that reshape a company.

What he is doing now

Tom founded T1 Consulting LLC to bring that experience to companies developing, manufacturing, and scaling physical products — across new product development, manufacturing strategy, category and channel leadership, and M&A advisory. He is supported on T1's engagements by Thomas M. Russo, who served as Vice President of Marketing and New Product Development for Newell Brands' Connected Home & Security division and led the launch of the OneLink Safe & Sound connected smoke + carbon-monoxide alarm.
